Onfly

A fast and easy info system for mobile

View project onGitHub

Onfly version 1

is a lightweight solution to share infos, messages, suggestions, skills or dictionaries; you can use it in your LAN or in Internet.

  • A fresh interface provides small suggestions, phone numbers and links on your smartphone or tablet.
  • An easy to use admin back-end (built on top of https://code.google.com/p/phpliteadmin/) helps to manage your messages.

Possible uses

Onfly has a wide range of possiblities, here you can find some samples:

  • Lorem ipsum
  • Events/Places management
  • An Hospital
  • A restaurant

The code is HTML5 with jQueryMobile and PHP, the database used is SQlite.
